Week 1 Reflection

Getting started with IML 300 and p5.js.

Reflection

In Week 1, I focused on setting up my tools and getting comfortable with the idea of using code as a medium. Writing simple p5.js sketches made me think about composition and timing in a different way than traditional design work.

I started to understand how the class frames experimentation as a core part of the process. Instead of aiming for a perfect final piece, I am trying to see each sketch as a small test. Changing parameters, colors, and motion helps me notice how each decision can shift the feeling of the screen.
