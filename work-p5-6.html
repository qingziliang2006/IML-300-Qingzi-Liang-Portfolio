p5.js 6

Description

This sketch plays with circular motion and orbiting forms. I wanted to create a sense of systems within systems, where each element responds to an invisible center or axis.

Working on this piece helped me understand how trigonometric functions in p5.js can be used to sculpt motion. It also opened up ideas for future interactive versions where the viewer could influence speed, direction, or the number of orbiting elements.
