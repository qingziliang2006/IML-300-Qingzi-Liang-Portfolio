Description

This sketch focuses on repetition and grid-based composition. I was interested in how a simple unit, when repeated across the canvas, can start to feel like a patterned environment rather than individual shapes.

By adjusting spacing, color, and small movements, I explored how to keep a grid from feeling static. The sketch is part of a larger exploration of structure versus randomness and how much order a generative system should have.
