p5.js 5

Description

In this sketch, I experimented with more expressive color palettes and subtle randomness in position. The aim was to let the sketch feel slightly unstable and alive while still remaining visually coherent.

This piece helped me think about the balance between control and chaos in generative art. Small variations from frame to frame can make the canvas feel like it is breathing, which is something I want to explore more in future work.
