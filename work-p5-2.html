Description

This sketch builds on my first p5.js experiment by introducing more variation in color and rhythm. I focused on adjusting timing and repetition to see how a simple pattern could feel more dynamic or calm depending on its speed and density.

It is an exercise in tweaking small parameters and paying attention to how they change the emotional quality of the screen. The sketch reflects an early stage in learning how to create atmosphere and mood through procedural visuals.
