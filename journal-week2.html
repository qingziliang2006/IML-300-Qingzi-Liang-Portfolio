Week 2 Reflection

Iterating on sketches and testing new tools.

Reflection

In Week 2, I began building on my initial sketches instead of starting from a blank canvas each time. Duplicating a sketch and changing one or two things helped me see how variation can emerge from a simple system.

I also started to connect p5.js with other tools, such as AI-generated moodboards and prompts, to guide my visual decisions. The main takeaway from this week is that being curious and breaking things on purpose can be more valuable than trying to keep everything polished and finished.
