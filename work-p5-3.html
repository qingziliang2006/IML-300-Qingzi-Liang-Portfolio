Description

This sketch experiments with layering and overlapping motion. The goal was to see how multiple elements moving at different speeds could create a sense of depth, tension, or visual noise on the canvas.

I used this piece to practice balancing complexity and clarity, making sure that the screen still felt readable even as more elements were introduced. It represents a step toward building more intricate interactive compositions in p5.js.
